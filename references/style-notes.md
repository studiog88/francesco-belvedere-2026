# Style Notes

## Mood keywords
- Quiet
- Editorial
- Intentional
- Tactile
- Restrained

## Visual direction
- Prioritize spacing rhythm over decorative effects.
- Keep typography contrast clear and readable.
- Use subtle color accents for action states only.

## Do
- Keep interactions minimal and purposeful.
- Favor consistent card patterns and section spacing.
- Preserve mobile readability first.

## Don't
- Add heavy animation before core layout and content are stable.
- Mix too many card or button styles in early versions.
- Compromise readability for visual novelty.
